RTX-AMMOS Field Drive Rack
==========================
A: floppy RTXBOOT (RTXDRV.SYS)
B: reserved
C: RTXDOS fixed disk (AMMOFAT.SYS)
D: CD-ROM ISO9660 (RTXCD.SYS + MSCDEX)
E: host bridge - drop files in assets/dos/incoming/host/

Shell: DRIVES | DIR A: | CD D: | MOUNT CD | TYPE FIELDLAY.TXT
